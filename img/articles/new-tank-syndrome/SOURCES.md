# Image sources — `new-tank-syndrome`

All WebP files in this folder were derived from photographs downloaded from **Unsplash** under the [Unsplash License](https://unsplash.com/license).

Editorial note: earlier placeholders showed open-ocean or marine reef scenes, which did not match a freshwater-focused article. The current assets are **home freshwater planted aquarium** photography.

| `mod-planted-720.webp`, `mod-planted-1200.webp` | **Christian Ang** ([@shots](https://unsplash.com/@shots)) | [A fish tank filled with plants and rocks](https://unsplash.com/photos/-gxn0RkXsG0) | `images.unsplash.com/photo-1691387896833-dba10ea7d614` |
